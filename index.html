Executive Summary – A Catalyst for Economic Inclusion

The Initiative

A multi-sectoral national platform designed to bridge the structural gap between Tanzania's youth workforce and economic opportunities through a flagship Expo and digital ecosystem.

The Objective

To unlock sustainable livelihoods by providing direct access to employers, skills development, policymakers, and innovation capital.

The Invitation

A call for Government, Private Sector, and Development Partners to co-create a scalable, inclusive engine for national prosperity.

Tanzania's Untapped Economic Engine

◆

The Asset

  • Youth represent the largest segment of the national workforce.
  • High digital literacy and entrepreneurial ambition.
◆

The Challenge

  • Skills-Market Disconnect: Education outputs do not match labor market needs.
  • Information Asymmetry: Limited access to data on growth sectors.
  • Access Barriers: High friction to reach decision-makers.
◆

The Gap Between Talent and Opportunity

  • Fragmented Information: Opportunities exist but remain invisible to youth outside elite networks.
  • Weak Employer-Youth Interface: Employers struggle to identify talent; youth lack venues to showcase potential.
  • Exclusionary Systems: Recruitment platforms often exclude marginalized groups and persons with disabilities.
